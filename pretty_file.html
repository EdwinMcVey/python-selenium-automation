Cream Cheese Potato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 1 Leek, cleaned and minced, white part only
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon season salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/8 – 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 15 ounces chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 5-6 Russet potatoes peeled and diced potatoes
  • 2 cups of 2% or skim milk
  • 4 ounces of cream cheese
  • Cooked crumbled bacon, shredded cheese and chopped green onion for each serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large dutch oven, heat oil over medium high heat. Add the onions, leek sauté until onions have softened. Stir in season salt, pepper and cayenne pepper.
  2. Add garlic to dutch over and sauté until aroma rises.
  3. Add chicken broth, potatoes and water and bring to boil. Simmer until potatoes are just tender (About 23 minutes).
  4. Ladle mixture into blender a few cups at a time. Add milk and cream to each ladle. Puree mixture on high. Set each purred batch into a big bowl.
  5. After all batches have been pureed, add purred potato soup back into to dutch oven to reheat.
  6. Reduce heat to medium. Continue to cook, until steam rises from soup. Serve with bacon, shredded cheese and chopped green onions.
    Makes 6-8 cups. Enjoy!
